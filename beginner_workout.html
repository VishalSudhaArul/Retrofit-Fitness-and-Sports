Beginner Workout Plan

Easy Start for Beginners

Get started with light exercises designed to improve strength and endurance.

Workout Plan

  • Warm-up: 5 min stretching
  • Jumping Jacks: 30 sec
  • Bodyweight Squats: 12 reps x 2 sets
  • Push-ups (Knee-supported): 10 reps x 2 sets
  • Plank Hold: 20 sec
  • Cool-down: 5 min light stretching
